Basic Details

  • Galway to Norway
  • 10th - 30th April 2016
  • Road Kilometers 6,516 - Round Trip
  • In aid of the charity Pieta House

    • Join this fundraising Overland journey to Norway. A total of ten Landrovers will begin this exciting journey to the beautiful region of Norway, covering a total of 6,516 kilometers in the month of April 2016. All sponsorship funds will benifit Pieta House.

    This Overland journey is open to any model of Landrover. The journey will be combination of road and ferry and will depart Galway City on April 10th 2016. All overnight stays will be a combination of wild camping or organised campsites, so all participants must be prepared adequately for this adventure. The return date to Ireland is April 30th 2016.

    Each individual is responsible for all their own costs, ferry bookings and campsite fees. All vehicles must be road worthy and insured for travel outside of Ireland.

    Of the ten vehicles traveling, 8 spaces are available currently and each vehicle will be required to raise 1000 euro in sponsorship to benifit Pieta House.

 

Ferry Costs

  • Ferry - Dublin to Holyhead 320 euro
  • Ferry - Harwich to Rotterdam 196 euro
  • Ferry - Hirtshals to Kristiansand 198 euro

    • These ferry rates are based on two passengers with a Landrover Defender on a return basis. These rates are for May 2015 as April 2016 are not available as of yet. If bookings are made in Dec of 2015,the Dublin - Holyhead leg should be cheaper. Group rates may also be available.

 

Diesel and Petrol Costs

  • England Diesel - 1.51 euro Petrol - 1.42 euro
  • Holland Diesel - 1.26 euro Petrol - 1.54 euro
  • Denmark Diesel - 1.28 euro Petrol - 1.43 euro
  • Norway Diesel - 1.50 euro Petrol - 1.61 euro

    • Diesel and Petrol prices for the countries that we will visit, these prices are from the date of Jan 25, 2015. Note that England and Norway are expensive for Diesel and Petrol, therefore the usage of jerry cans and filling before entering the UK and Norway is recommended
