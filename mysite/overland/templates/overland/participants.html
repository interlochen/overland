Participants

Seamus Dooley

Seamus and his 1993 Land Rover Defender 110, Seamus is the main event organizer, and resides in Craughwell, County Galway. Seamus spent many years off-roading in the deserts and mountains of California. Seamus rebuilt his Defender 110 over a two year period, starting with a new galvanized chassie. Main additions to the vehicle include, rooftop tent, side awning, Discovery rear axle, Discovery front seats, and Exmoor trim second row seats. The vehicle features original engine and gear box. Seamus's occupation is in hotel management. Seamus is married with 5 children.

 

Ryan Dooley

Ryan Dooley, son of Seamus Dooley, resides in Arizona currently attending Northern Arizona Unviersity. Ryan will be documenting the trip and keeping followers up to date on the blog. Ryan created the expedition website.

 

Val Lynam

Val is the Chef proprietor of the award winning Green Olive Catering in Galway. Val has travelled extensively; previous expeditions include cycling across Cambodia, cycling from the base of Kilimanjaro to the Angora Basin, cycling across northern India, trekking across Mexico. Val uses his Land rover Defender as a key "workhorse" in the running of his business on event sites across Ireland. Val is married with three children.

 

Terry Gaffney

Val Lynam's brother in law, Terry, is a carpenter based in Moycullen, Co Galway. He is married with two children. Terry is a keen amateur mechanic having restored a classic 1992 Mini Sprint. Terry has had some interesting travelling experiences throughout Australasia, but his most memorable has to be a six week tour of Europe in a 1989 Nissan Micra. Norway in a Defender will be a walk in the park!

 

Garret and Deirdre

Owners of the multi award winning The Pigs Tale Restaurant in Gorey. Garret is the Chef and Deirdre runs front of house. They love to travel and explore new countries. Garret also loves to hunt and shoot and is passionate about wild food and cooking. Between them their previous Landrovers have been a P38 2.5 DSE + 4.6 HSE , Defender 90 1989, Range Rover Classic 1986 3.5 V8, Discovery TD5 2002, Freelander 2002, Defender TD5 Crew Cab 2004 and currently a 2005 Defender Utility. Garret enjoys maintaining his Defender and has carried out all maintenance and major improvements himself.

 

John Reel

The director of Automagic.ie the high end name in car detailing product line in Ireland, John has lived in both the U.S.A and the Middle East for a number of years, and currently lives in Boyle co Roscommon. He has varied interests including horses, hunting, working on his Land Rovers, and participating in worth while expeditions for good causes.

 

Anthony O Reilly

Anthony resides in Galway and will be taking his 110 defender. This 2011 defender is equipped with solar power on the roof with an inverter that can power Burko catering water boilers. He has installed leather seat backs from Audi that are modified to fit defender seat bases.

 

Declan Gallen

Declan is from Letterkenny in Co Donegal and will be travelling with his recently acquired 2011 TDCi Defender 110.He manages his own vending machine company and is married with four boys.Declan has always had an interest in Land Rovers and owns a 2002 TD5 90 as well which he uses for greenlaning.At present he is in the process of modifying his 110 for the upcoming trip in March.
