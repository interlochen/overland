Participants

Seamus Dooley

Seamus and his 1993 Land Rover Defender 110, Seamus is the main event organizer, and resides in Craughwell, County Galway. Seamus spent many years off-roading in the deserts and mountains of California. Seamus rebuilt his Defender 110 over a two year period, starting with a new galvanized chassie. Main additions to the vehicle include, rooftop tent, side awning, discovery rear axle, discovery front seats, and exmoor trim second row seats. The vehicle features original engine and gear box. Seamus's occupation is in hotel management. Seamus is married with 5 children.

 

Ryan Dooley

Ryan Dooley, son of Seamus Dooley, resides in Arizona currently attending Northern Arizona Unviersity. Ryan will be documenting the trip and keeping followers up to date on the blog. Ryan created the expedition website.

 

Val Lynam

Val is the Chef proprietor of the award winning Green Olive catering in Galway. Val has travelled extensively; previous expeditions include cycling across Cambodia; cycling from the base of Kilimanjaro to the Angora angora basin; cycling across northern India; trekking across Mexico. Val uses his Land rover defender as a key "workhorse" in the running of his business on event sites across Ireland. Val is married with three children.

 

Terry Gaffney

Val Lynam's brother in law, Terry, is a carpenter based in Moycullen, Co Galway. He is married with two children. Terry is a keen amateur mechanic having restored a classic 1992 mini sprite. Terry has had some interesting travelling experiences throughout Australasia, but his most memorable has to be a six week tour of Europe in a 1989 Nissan Micra. Norway in a defender will be a walk in the park!

 

Garret and Deirdre

We own the multi award winning The Pigs Tale Restaurant in Gorey. I am the Chef and Deirdre runs front of house. We love to travel and explore new countries. I also love to hunt and shoot and im passionate about wild food and cooking. Between us our previous Landrovers have been a P38 2.5 DSE + 4.6 HSE , Defender 90 1989, Range Rover Classic 1986 3.5 V8, Discovery TD5 2002, Freelander 2002, Defender TD5 Crew Cab 2004 and currently a 2005 Defender Utility. I enjoy maintaining my Defender and have carried out all maintenance and major improvements myself.
